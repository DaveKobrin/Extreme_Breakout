Welcome to Extreme Breakout!

Instructions:

To play move the paddle across the bottom of the game window to keep the ball from falling off the bottom of the screen.

Earn points by bouncing the ball into the bricks above and the alien invader as well.

Be carefull of the stun bombs that the alien shoots down at you, they will prevent you from moving for 2 seconds which could cause you to miss the ball!

Also, don't forget to catch any power-ups that you knock loose!

Good Luck!

  • Move Left: Left Arrow
  • Move Right: Right Arrow
  • Launch Ball: Space Bar
  • Pause: P
  • Instructions:I
  • Quit: Q
